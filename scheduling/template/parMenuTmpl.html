Annual Physical

It is time for me to get a annual check-up. Note: Since most insurances will only cover one physical a year, we ask that you please schedule physicals at least a year plus one day from your last one. Please visit your appointment history if you aren't sure when your last one was performed. You may see the appointment reason as Physical or Annual Visit or CEX (our short-hand for complete exam). If in doubt, check with us first before scheduling. Thank you!

Sick Visit

I am sick and I would like to see my doctor today, or as soon as possible. NOTE: If you are experiencing a medical emergency, please call 911.

Wellness Visit

I am a MEDICARE patient and would like to schedule my Annual Wellness Visit. Note: Annual Wellness Visits (also know as an AWV) for Medicare patients MUST be scheduled a year apart. If you do not know if and/or when you had your last AWV, please check with us first before scheduling. Any of these visits scheduled less than a year apart will have to be rescheduled.

Worker's Compensation

I need to see my provider about a specific work-related injury. NOTE: Please be sure that you have filed your worker's compensation case number with your employer prior to your appointment. Failure to provide the claim information may result in full payment being expected at the time of your visit.
