Days in Accounts Receivable

The Days in Accounts Receivable (DAR) Trend widget tracks the historical pattern of DAR and AR balance. DAR is calculated by using the month end total AR divided by total charges for the most recent 3 months divided by 91. Vertical bars represent the selected metric at the end of each month (labeled in the horizontal axis), the yellow line represents the trend line as defined by the trend drop-down in the toolbar, and red diamonds represent benchmarks. View by days in AR (DAR) or AR dollars (AR$).


Denials

The Denial Trend widget displays posted denials based on Month of Transaction. A charge is considered denied when an actionable rejection code is posted to it. Vertical bars represent denied charges posted in the month, the yellow line represents the trend line as defined by the trend drop-down in the toolbar, and red diamonds represent benchmarks. View by charge dollars (chg) or volume (vol).
